# LilyFit

Your personal fitness and nutrition companion that helps you achieve your health goals through intelligent calorie management, macro tracking, and progress monitoring.

## What is LilyFit?

LilyFit is a comprehensive fitness and nutrition tracking app designed to help you take control of your health journey. Whether you want to lose weight, build muscle, or maintain your current physique, LilyFit provides personalized guidance and easy-to-use tools to help you succeed.

## Key Features

### Personalized Health Goals

Set your health objectives and let LilyFit calculate your daily targets:
- **Weight Loss** - Create a sustainable calorie deficit to shed pounds
- **Muscle Gain** - Get adequate calories and protein for building muscle
- **Weight Maintenance** - Maintain your ideal weight with balanced nutrition

Your personalized plan considers your age, gender, current weight, height, and activity level to provide accurate calorie and macro targets.

### Smart Nutrition Tracking

**Calorie & Macro Tracking**
- Log your meals throughout the day (breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks)
- Track not just calories, but also protein, carbohydrates, and fats
- See your daily progress with intuitive visual indicators

**Comprehensive Food Database**
- Search from a vast database of foods including regional and international cuisines
- Find African dishes, global favorites, and common foods
- Quick search functionality to log meals in seconds

### Hydration Monitoring

- Track your daily water intake
- Set personalized water goals based on your needs
- Receive smart reminders throughout the day to stay hydrated
- Monitor your hydration streaks

### Weight Progress Tracking

- Log your weight regularly
- Visualize your progress with beautiful charts and graphs
- Track your BMI and understand your categorization
- See trends over time to stay motivated

### Progress Analytics

- View detailed statistics and insights about your journey
- Track your consistency with streak counters
- Analyze patterns in your eating and hydration habits
- Celebrate your achievements with milestone tracking

### Multilingual Support

Access LilyFit in your preferred language:
-  English
-  Spanish
-  French
-  German
-  Portuguese

### Customizable Experience

- **Unit Preferences**: Choose between Metric (kg/cm) or Imperial (lbs/ft) units
- **Dark Mode**: Comfortable viewing experience day or night
- **Activity Levels**: Select from sedentary to very active lifestyles
- **Smart Notifications**: Customize reminders for water intake and meal logging

## How It Works

1. **Get Started**: Create your account and complete a simple onboarding process
2. **Set Your Goals**: Tell us about yourself and what you want to achieve
3. **Track Daily**: Log your meals, water intake, and weight regularly
4. **Monitor Progress**: Check your dashboard to see how you're doing
5. **Achieve Results**: Stay consistent and reach your health goals

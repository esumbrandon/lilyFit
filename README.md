# LilyFit 🍽️

A smart calorie and nutrition management app that helps users manage their calorie intake based on their health goals - whether they want to lose weight, gain weight, or maintain their current weight.

## Features

- 📊 **Calorie Tracking** - Track your daily calorie intake
- 🥗 **Macro Monitoring** - Monitor protein, carbs, and fat
- 🌍 **Global Food Database** - Includes African cuisines and global foods
- 🎯 **Personalized Goals** - Customized calorie targets based on your goals
- 💧 **Water Tracking** - Stay hydrated with daily water goals
- 📈 **Progress Tracking** - Monitor weight changes over time
- 🔐 **Secure Authentication** - Powered by Supabase
